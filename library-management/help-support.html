Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?

Go to Password & Security in your profile settings, enter your current password, and choose a new one.

How do I delete my account?

Navigate to Password & Security, then under Account Management select Delete Account.

How do I add a book to Continue Reading?

Open a book from the Dashboard or Library and click "Resume" (or the add-to-continue action). The book is saved locally and will sync to your account when you sign in.

Why are my favourites not syncing across devices?

Favourites sync requires you to be signed in. If you are signed in and still see a mismatch, check your network connection and ensure the app has permission to use storage. You can also try signing out and back in to force a sync or check the browser console for errors.

How do I add or remove a book from My Favourites?

Click the heart icon button on any book card in the Dashboard to add it. To remove, open My Favourites and use the "Remove" button on the card. If you're signed in, changes sync to your account; otherwise they are stored locally in your browser.
