Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?

Go to Password & Security in your profile settings, enter your current password, and choose a new one.

How do I delete my account?

Navigate to Password & Security, then under Account Management select Delete Account.

Why can't I access a book in My Library?

Some books may have access restrictions. Ensure you are logged in or contact support for assistance.

How can I upload or request a book?

You can use the Upload Book option under My Library or contact us for special requests.
